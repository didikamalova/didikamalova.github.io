Sequence Pillbox

  • Tools: Arduino, ESP8266, AutoCAD, Swift

A smart pillbox equipped with remote monitoring features designed to help patients stick to their medication routines. It uses automated reminders, instant notifications, and online tracking for families. Caregivers can easily set up reminders for upcoming doses through a mobile app. These reminders will then be sent as audio and visual reminders from the pillbox, making it easy to remember.

Whenever a patient takes a pill from the box, even if they take the wrong dose or miss a dose, the system will immediately notify the caregiver in real-time. The pillbox's technology includes the ESP-07 Wi-Fi module, weight sensors, and light resistant sensors, all built into a custom circuit board.
