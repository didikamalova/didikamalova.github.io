2D Persistence of Vision (POV) Bike Wheel

  • Tools: Arduino

Two strips of DotStar LEDs are affixed to the spokes of a bike wheel, one facing each direction, driven by a 5V Pro Trinket and powered by a waterproof 3xAA battery pack to create a persistent (almost) image on a bike wheel. I have assembled this circuit and used the code from this repo inspired by an Adafruit tutorial!
