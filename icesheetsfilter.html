Ice Sheets Noise Filter

  • Tools: Python, Signal Processing, MATLAB
  • Project URL: GitHub

A dynamic bandstop filter to attenuate noise on the corrupted real data from the South Pole 2023 campaign. Achieved a 36.36dB SNR with a three-segment parabolic-arc bandstop filter & power spectral density analysis. One of the approaches that I implemented involve varying bandwidth according to 6 bounding curves applied to three time segments.
